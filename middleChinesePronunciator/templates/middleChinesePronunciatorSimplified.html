This made is made by Cary Wang; the goal is to have a first shot of knowing how pronunciators are created. Since no one has created a middle Chinese pronunciator, I see this as a pretty good shot to make that happen, using a website.

The idea is to type the Chinese words you would like to pronounce in middle Chinese, to your left. After typing the words, the words that the middle Chinese words have processed are to be printed to the textbox to the right(and like the cantonese pronunciator, whatever words that are not able to be pronounced will be marked with blue). From there, once you press "Play", the word/word(s) will be pronounced in middle chinese.
